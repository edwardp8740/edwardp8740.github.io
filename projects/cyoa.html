CYOA, Choose Your Own Adventure

This project is a choose your own adventure game. Feel free to try the game out!

Context

This project was assigned to us by our teacher to help us with linking files, but it also helped us practice collaboration with others that we didn't know.

Challenges

Â I think the biggest challenge in this was the collaboration because we were also assigned people we didn't know to show us how it would feel like collaborating with others we wouldn't know in the future. Me and my partner overcame this by just simpling going out of our comfort zone and talking. This allowed us to work better and faster than other groups.

Takeaways

If I had more times I would definitely try to add more and continue the story as much as I could. Something I would do differently would be to just start talking because it became really easy that way.
